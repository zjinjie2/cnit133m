Characteristic of PWAs

Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) are web applications that leverage modern web technologies to provide a reliable, fast, and engaging user experience.
Here are some key characteristics of PWAs:

  • Works Everywhere: A Progressive Web App (PWA) is like a website that works on any device, whether it's a computer, tablet, or phone.
  • Fits Your Screen: It looks good and is easy to use no matter how big or small your device's screen is.
  • Works Offline: Even if your internet is slow or not working, a PWA can still show you some stuff you've seen before.
  • Feels Like an App: It acts like an app you'd download, with smooth movements and cool features.
  • Updates Itself: It can make itself better without you having to do anything.
